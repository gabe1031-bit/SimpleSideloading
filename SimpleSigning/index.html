Simple Signing

Welcome to SimpleSigning! This website has the philosophy of keeping things simple for legacy iOS devices. This part of SimpleBit handles signing any IPAs that you have (using our own/your collection of enterprise certs). We might accept AppleID or Ad-hoc certificates, but it may not work reliably.

The name being a default (SimpleSigning...) is a known bug.

This method is in beta, and things may not work. If you want to contribute, just ask. Give some feedback in issues. Thanks!
