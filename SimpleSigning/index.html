Simple Signing

Welcome to SimpleSigning! This website has the philosophy of keeping things simple for legacy iOS devices. This part of SimpleBit handles signing any IPAs that you have (using our own/your collection of enterprise certs). We would NOT accept AppleID or Ad-hoc certificates that require UDID to be signed into the app.
